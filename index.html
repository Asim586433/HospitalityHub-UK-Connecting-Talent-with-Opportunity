About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, HospitalityHub UK has been connecting talented professionals with top employers in the UK hospitality industry for over a decade.

Our team of experienced recruiters specializes in all areas of hospitality, from Michelin-starred restaurants to boutique hotels and event venues.

We pride ourselves on our personalized approach, building long-term relationships with both candidates and employers to ensure the perfect match every time.
